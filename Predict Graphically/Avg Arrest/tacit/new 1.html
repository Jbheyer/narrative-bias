In the following visualization you will be asked questions regarding *drug related arrest rates within a county that has Opioid Treatment Programs (OTPs).

The first county is:

  • St. Joseph County:
    • 2017 population of 270,434
    • 1 Opioid Treatment Program
    • 5,889 average arrests over 2016-2017
    • Opened in 1988-1990

An opioid treatment program (OTP) is defined as “a program or practitioner engaged in opioid treatment of individuals with an opioid agonist medication” [1].

  • *Average arrests include general, drug and opioid-related arrests
  • *Drug related arrests exclude general
  • *There are no duplicate arrests
